Why Green Minds?

Farming requires immense dedication, hard work, and resilience. Yet, farmers worldwide face unpredictable weather, financial instability, crop failure, and market fluctuations. These ongoing pressures deeply affect their mental health.

Many farmers experience stress, anxiety, depression, and sadly, sometimes suicidal thoughts. Due to stigma, isolation, and limited mental health services in rural areas, these problems often go unaddressed.

Supporting farmers’ mental well-being is crucial. Increasing awareness, providing access to counseling, and building strong support communities can save lives. Governments and agricultural bodies must prioritize this. A healthy farmer is just as important as a healthy crop.
